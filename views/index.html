THADOMAL SHAHANI ENGINEERING COLLEGE

About TSEC

Principal: Dr. G.T. Thampi

Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC) is a premier engineering institution in Mumbai, established in 1983. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines, known for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life. TSEC is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and is recognized for fostering innovation and technical expertise among students.
Dr. G. T. Thampi, the principal of TSEC, is a well-respected academician and leader in the field of engineering education. He is known for his emphasis on holistic student development, combining technical knowledge with soft skills, ethics, and social responsibility. Under his leadership, TSEC has grown in academic stature and community engagement.

UPCOMING EVENTS

Codesiaance: Codestorm Hackathon

The Premier Hackathon by Codestorm Committee Codesiaance is an innovative hackathon organized by TSEC's Codestorm Committee, bringing together tech enthusiasts to solve real-world challenges. Participants engage in collaborative problem-solving, showcasing their coding prowess and creativity through cutting-edge projects.

Shazam: Sporting Extravaganza

TSEC's Ultimate Multi-Sporting Extravaganza Shazam is a thrilling multiple sporting event conducted by the TSEC Student Council. It features a wide array of competitive sports, promoting camaraderie and sportsmanship among students while fostering a vibrant campus spirit.

NFC: CodeTantra's Coding Challenge

A Dynamic Hackathon by CodeTantra Need for Code (NFC) is a fast-paced hackathon powered by CodeTantra, providing a platform for coders to demonstrate their programming skills. The event focuses on innovative software solutions, encouraging participants to push the boundaries of their technical expertise.

TSEC Hacks: Innovation Marathon

A Tech Innovation Marathon by Codecell Organized by the Codecell Committee of TSEC, TSEC Hacks is a 24-hour hackathon that challenges participants to create innovative software solutions. This event fosters creativity, teamwork, and the application of emerging technologies to address modern challenges
